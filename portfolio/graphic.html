Graphic Design


Since starting at Washington State University, my graphic skills have also been expanding. I didn't really intend to focus on them, but it dawned on me that graphic design was something I held an interest in, and something that I enjoyed.


I've been drawing digitally since 2006, but my skills were limited to Paint Tool Sai, a popular sketching and inking application. 2014 marks the first year that I've been designing digitally, as well as drawing.
